Our team at the University of Florida, including Cheng Zeng, Jirui Jin, and Mingjie Liu, has been honored with the HiPerGator Pioneering Team Award for our work on developing advanced graph neural network techniques to explore the vast chemical space. This recognition, awarded by the UF Artificial Intelligence and Informatics Research Institute, highlights the impact of combining high-performance computing with cutting-edge AI for chemistry and materials discovery. The official news release can be found [here](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/uf-department-of-chemistry_ai-hipergator-ugcPost-7391560673027072000-N7DR/?utm_source=social_share_send&utm_medium=member_desktop_web&rcm=ACoAABXPtG4Bs-u6fGeXmbmPt4C7kd1oF6BsC-w).
